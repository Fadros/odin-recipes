Steps

  • Cook sausage, beef, onions and garlic over medium heat
  • Stir in crushed tomatoes, sauce, paste, and water
  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodle
  • Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  • Assemble spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 ½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
